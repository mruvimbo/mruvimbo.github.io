About Me

Welcome to my page. I’m Ruvimbo Mashinge, a passionate data analytics and cybersecurity enthusiast. I am particularly interested in the intersection between data-driven insights and the critical importance of securing that data.

This realization sparked my interest in combining Data Analytics with Cybersecurity, enabling me to develop a unique and complementary skill set.
